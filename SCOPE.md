# SCOPE: The End-to-End Content Engine

## 1. Project Overview
An integrated, multi-agent AI system that automates the entire content marketing lifecycle—from identifying high-value SEO opportunities to generating a complete, multi-channel campaign with a single click.

### Stakeholders
- **FSA Technical Owner**: [FDE Name]
- **Executive Sponsor**: [C-Suite Title]
- **Target Audience**: Marketing Team, SEO Specialists.

---

## 2. Business Problem & Objectives

### The Pain Point
- **Slow & Disconnected**: Marketing teams spend weeks on manual research, missing key trends.
- **Low ROI**: Once published, blog posts often sit idle due to the manual effort required for multi-channel promotion.

### C-Suite Objectives
- **Increase Organic Traffic**: Target 25% growth.
- **Velocity**: Double the speed of launching promotional campaigns.
- **Agility**: Transition from disconnected steps to a "single, agile content machine."

---

## 3. Success Criteria & KPIs
- [ ] 25% increase in organic search traffic within 6 months of deployment.
- [ ] 50% reduction in time taken to launch a multi-channel campaign (from blog post to social/email/ad assets).
- [ ] Automated generation of "Campaign-in-a-Box" for 100% of new priority content.
